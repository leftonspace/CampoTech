## Executive Summary

### The Core Problem
CampoTech solves a chicken-and-egg marketplace problem:
- **Without businesses** → Consumers open the app, see nothing, delete it forever
- **Without consumers** → Businesses see no value, won't pay

## Market Analysis

### Argentina Service Industry Context

| Factor | Reality | Opportunity |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Informal Economy** | ~40% of workers are informal | Formalization tools (AFIP integration) |
| **WhatsApp Dominance** | 95%+ business communication via WhatsApp | WhatsApp-first notifications |
| **Trust Issues** | Hard to find reliable service providers | Verified reviews & ratings |
| **Price Sensitivity** | Economic instability, peso devaluation | Flexible ARS pricing |
| **Aging Workforce** | Older tradespeople struggle with tech | Simple, AI-assisted tools |
| **Competition** | IguanaFix charges 15% commission | FREE for consumers |

### Target Market Size (Buenos Aires)

| Category | Estimated Businesses | Addressable Market |
|----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Plumbers (Plomeros) | ~8,000 | Primary target |
| Electricians (Electricistas) | ~10,000 | Phase B |
| HVAC (Aire Acondicionado) | ~5,000 | Phase B |
| Locksmiths (Cerrajeros) | ~3,000 | Phase B |
| Painters (Pintores) | ~6,000 | Phase C+ |
| Construction (Albañiles) | ~15,000 | Phase C+ |
| Gas Fitters (Gasistas) | ~4,000 | Phase C+ |
| **Total Addressable** | **~51,000** | |

### Why Start with Plumbers?

1. **High Demand:** Everyone needs plumbers (emergencies, leaks, installations)
2. **Repeat Business:** Plumbing issues recur, creating ongoing value
3. **Clear Pain Points:** Scheduling, invoicing, customer communication
4. **Network Effects:** Plumbers know other tradespeople (referrals)
5. **Visible Results:** Easy to photograph before/after work
6. **Emergency Nature:** High urgency = high perceived value of quick matching

## Pricing Model by Phase

### Pricing Philosophy

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                         PRICING PRINCIPLES                                  │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                             │
│  1. START LOW → BUILD HABIT → INCREASE WITH VALUE                           │
│     Free trials create adoption, paid features create revenue               │
│                                                                             │
│  2. AFFORDABLE FOR SOLO WORKERS                                             │
│     A 55-year-old plumber working alone should afford the basic tier        │
│     Target: ~2-3 hours of work per month covers subscription                │
│                                                                             │
│  3. SCALABLE FOR GROWING BUSINESSES                                         │
│     Larger businesses pay more for more features (fair)                     │
│                                                                             │
│  4. LEADS ARE THE PREMIUM                                                   │
│     Access to consumer marketplace = highest value = highest tier           │
│                                                                             │
│  5. PESO-DENOMINATED, INFLATION-ADJUSTED                                    │
│     Review pricing quarterly to match economic reality                      │
│                                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Phase A Pricing (Months 1-6) - Foundation

| Tier | Price (ARS/month) | Price (USD equiv.) | Features |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Founding Member** | FREE | $0 | All features for 3 months |
| **Básico** | $5,000 | ~$5 | Job management, invoicing, 1 user |

**Goal:** Get adoption, not revenue. Prove the product works.

### Phase B Pricing (Months 7-9) - Growth

| Tier | Price (ARS/month) | Price (USD equiv.) | Features |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Básico** | $7,500 | ~$7.50 | Job management, invoicing, 1 user |
| **Profesional** | $15,000 | ~$15 | + Team (3 users), tracking, reminders |
| **Empresarial** | $25,000 | ~$25 | + Unlimited users, advanced analytics |

**Goal:** Establish willingness to pay. Segment the market.

### Phase C Pricing (Months 10-12) - Marketplace

| Tier | Price (ARS/month) | Price (USD equiv.) | Features |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Básico** | $10,000 | ~$10 | Job management, invoicing, public profile |
| **Profesional** | $25,000 | ~$25 | + Team, tracking, VIEW consumer leads |
| **Crecimiento** | $45,000 | ~$45 | + RECEIVE leads (up to 20/month), respond to quotes |
| **Empresarial** | $75,000 | ~$75 | + Unlimited leads, priority placement, verified badge |

**Goal:** Monetize the marketplace. Leads = premium value.

### Phase D+ Pricing (Year 2) - Mature Market

| Tier | Price (ARS/month) | Price (USD equiv.) | Features |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|
| **Básico** | $15,000 | ~$15 | Core tools only |
| **Profesional** | $35,000 | ~$35 | + Team, tracking, 10 leads/month |
| **Crecimiento** | $65,000 | ~$65 | + 30 leads/month, priority search |
| **Empresarial** | $120,000 | ~$120 | + Unlimited leads, featured, analytics |
| **Premium** | $200,000 | ~$200 | + Dedicated support, custom integrations |

### Pricing Comparison: CampoTech vs Competition

| Platform | Business Cost | Consumer Cost | Lead Model |
|----------|--------------|---------------|------------|
| **IguanaFix** | Free | 10-15% commission | Pay per transaction |
| **MercadoLibre Servicios** | Variable | Free | Pay per lead |
| **Word of Mouth/WhatsApp** | Free | Free | No leads |
| **CampoTech** | $10-75K ARS/month | FREE | Subscription + leads |

**CampoTech Advantage:**
- Businesses get comprehensive tools (not just leads)
- Consumers pay NOTHING (removes friction)
- Predictable monthly cost (no per-lead surprise charges)

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Slow plumber adoption | Medium | High | Aggressive door-to-door, FREE tier |
| Economic crisis (peso crash) | High | Medium | Flexible pricing, pause not cancel |
| Competition copies model | Medium | Medium | First-mover advantage, network effects |
| Consumer app flops | Medium | High | Only launch when 500+ businesses ready |
| Tech adoption resistance | High | Medium | Simple UX, human support, AI assistance |
| Regulatory changes (AFIP) | Low | High | Stay compliant, adapt quickly |
